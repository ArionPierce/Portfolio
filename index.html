About Arion

Life started in the Hoosier state better known as Indiana. I'd like to think that my life is more than just corn. I've always been super adventurous since I was able to walk. I'd run outside on a daily and make sure all of the evil villians were defeated before my mom called me in before dinner. Having traveled to more than 15 countries I'd like to say I still hold that adventurous title. Exploring the world brings excitement to my life. I like trying new foods, learning different cultures, rollerskating, cooking, and making others happy.
